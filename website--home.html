Why do I need SiteShot?

Checking your website for visual changes across multiple browsers, resolutions, sizes is a time consuming process that can take hours. SiteShot is designed to remove that process, and let you deploy with confidence.

How does SiteShot work?

SiteShot takes a picture of selected pages from your website and compares them between with past versions.

If any differences are spotted, SiteShot will then highlight the changes, and inform you what has changed.
